verified First Gynae-Oncologist in Dakshina Kannada About the Doctor

A pioneer in gynaecologic oncology for coastal Karnataka.

Dr. Mariam Anjum Ifthikar joined Yenepoya Specialty Hospital as a Visiting Consultant in December 2019, becoming the first recognised Gynaecologic Oncologist in Mangalore and Dakshina Kannada.

With formal training in chemotherapy for gynae-oncological cancers, palliative oncology, preventive oncology, and advanced onco-pelvic surgeries, she brings together surgical precision, oncology expertise, and a deeply compassionate approach to women's cancer care.

Her practice spans the full spectrum of benign gynaecological disease and gynaecologic malignancy — backed by a fellowship from Kidwai Memorial Institute of Oncology and an MCh from Amrita Institute of Medical Sciences in Breast, Robotic & Gynaecologic Oncology.

Frequently Asked

Patient Information.

Common questions about consultations, treatment, and what to expect.

How do I book an appointment with Dr. Anjum? expand_more

You can book an appointment by calling +91 6364 758 855 between 9:00 AM and 6:00 PM. Dr. Anjum sees patients at Yenepoya Specialty Hospital between 9:00 AM and 10:00 AM, Monday through Saturday.

What conditions does Dr. Anjum treat? expand_more

Dr. Anjum offers comprehensive care for ovarian, uterine, cervical, vulvar, and vaginal cancers, as well as molar pregnancy (GTD) and pre-invasive gynaecological conditions. She also treats benign conditions including fibroids, adenomyosis, endometriosis, and menstrual disorders.

Does Dr. Anjum perform robotic surgery? expand_more

Yes — Dr. Anjum is trained in advanced robotic onco-pelvic surgery, including Firefly sentinel pelvic lymphadenectomy, having completed her MCh residency at Amrita Institute of Medical Sciences. She is also fellowship-trained in minimal-access laparoscopic and hysteroscopic surgery (FMAS).

Do you offer HPV vaccination and cervical cancer screening? expand_more

Yes. Dr. Anjum offers HPV vaccination counselling and administration, colposcopy, and FOGSI-accredited cervical cytology services for the prevention and early detection of cervical cancer.

What should I bring to my first consultation? expand_more

Please bring all previous medical records, imaging studies (USG, CT, MRI, PET scans), pathology and biopsy reports, prescription history, and a list of current medications. If you have been treated elsewhere, please bring discharge summaries and operative notes if available.
