verified First Gynae-Oncologist & First Lady Robotic Surgeon in Dakshina Kannada About the Doctor

A pioneer in gynaecologic oncology for coastal Karnataka.

Dr. Mariam Anjum Ifthikar is the first recognised Gynaecologic Oncologist in Mangalore and Dakshina Kannada, and the first lady robotic surgeon performing robotic and advanced laparoscopic gynaecological surgeries in Mangalore and Dakshina Kannada — having performed over 1,000 robotic and 5,000 laparoscopic surgeries. She is also a proctor, mentor and trainer for budding robotic and laparoscopic surgeons.

With formal training in chemotherapy for gynae-oncological cancers, palliative oncology, preventive oncology, and advanced onco-pelvic surgeries, she brings together surgical precision, oncology expertise, and a deeply compassionate approach to women's cancer care.

Her practice spans the full spectrum of benign and complex gynaecological disease and gynaecologic malignancy — backed by a fellowship from Kidwai Memorial Institute of Oncology and an MCh from Amrita Institute of Medical Sciences in Breast, Robotic & Gynaecologic Oncology.

Frequently Asked

Patient Information.

Common questions about consultations, treatment, and what to expect.

How do I book an appointment with Dr. Anjum? expand_more

You can book an appointment by calling +91 6364 758 855 or the Yenepoya landline +91 824 423 8855 between 9:00 AM and 6:00 PM. Dr. Anjum consults between 8:30 AM and 3:30 PM at Zulekha Yenepoya Institute of Oncology (ZIO), Yenepoya Medical College Hospital, Deralakatte; and between 9:00 and 11:00 AM on Tuesdays, Thursdays and Saturdays at the Labour Room OPD, Yenepoya Specialty Hospital, Kodialbail.

What conditions does Dr. Anjum treat? expand_more

Dr. Anjum offers comprehensive care for ovarian, uterine, cervical, vulvar, and vaginal cancers, as well as molar pregnancy (GTD) and pre-invasive gynaecological conditions. She also treats benign conditions including fibroids, adenomyosis, endometriosis, and menstrual disorders.

Does Dr. Anjum perform laparoscopic and robotic surgery? expand_more

Yes. Dr. Anjum is trained in laparoscopic and robotic onco-pelvic surgery, and has mentored young doctors for laparoscopic and robotic surgeries and trained young surgeons for laparoscopic and robotic procedures. She also offers clinical observership for early surgeons keen on learning minimally invasive and open pelvic surgeries.

Do you offer HPV vaccination and cervical cancer screening? expand_more

Yes. Dr. Anjum offers HPV vaccination counselling and administration, colposcopy and cervical cancer screening for the prevention and early detection of cervical cancer.

What should I bring to my first consultation? expand_more

Please bring all previous medical records, imaging studies (USG, CT, MRI, PET scans), pathology and biopsy reports, prescription history, and a list of current medications. If you have been treated elsewhere, please bring discharge summaries and operative notes if available.
